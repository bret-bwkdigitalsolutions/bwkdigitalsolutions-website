About

A passion for solving problems

And a bird that started it all.

Meet Bret

I'm the founder of BWK Digital Solutions, and I believe technology should make your life easier — not harder.

Too often, I see businesses struggling with digital tools that were supposed to help them. Software that doesn't talk to each other. Websites that look outdated or don't work on mobile. Hours lost to tasks that could be automated.

It doesn't have to be this way.

About the bird

You've probably noticed the bird. Here's the thing — it's all I can draw. Doesn't matter what I try: a cow, a dog, a horse. They all end up looking like a bird.

So a long time ago, I gave up fighting it and perfected my bird instead. That bird now adorns the walls of bars and restaurants around the world, drawn wherever I was encouraged to sign the wall.

It's a pretty good metaphor for how I work, too. Stop forcing things that aren't working. Find what you're actually good at. Perfect that instead.

My Approach

I take the time to understand your actual problems before proposing solutions. There's no one-size-fits-all in digital solutions — what works for one business might be overkill or completely wrong for another.

  • Listening first, then solving
  • Practical solutions over flashy ones
  • Teaching you how things work, not creating dependency
  • Building systems that grow with your business

Based in Arkansas, working everywhere

BWK Digital Solutions is based in Little Rock, Arkansas, but digital work knows no boundaries. I work with clients locally and remotely, bringing the same level of attention and care to every project.
